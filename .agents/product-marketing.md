# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-05-18*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Zafhira is a luxury jewelry e-commerce platform offering premium collections with a sophisticated aesthetic.
**What it does:** It allows customers to browse and purchase high-end jewelry online, while providing administrators with a "Bóveda" dashboard to manage inventory, customer orders, and checkout processes securely.
**Product category:** E-commerce / Jewelry
**Product type:** B2C E-commerce Platform
**Business model:** Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) Retail

## Target Audience
**Target customers:** Individuals looking for luxury jewelry, premium gifts, and elegant accessories.
**Primary use case:** Purchasing high-quality jewelry online with a secure and premium shopping experience.
**Jobs to be done:** 
- Find exclusive jewelry pieces for special occasions.
- Purchase luxury items with confidence in quality and security.

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Finding authentic, luxury jewelry online with a trustworthy and seamless checkout experience.
**Why alternatives fall short:** Many online jewelry stores lack a premium aesthetic, have clunky checkout processes, or lack robust user account management.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- "The Velvet Gallery" premium design aesthetic.
- Seamless, modern user experience built on Next.js and Supabase.
- Secure customer accounts and order management.

## Goals
**Business goal:** Increase online sales, improve customer retention, and establish Zafhira as a premium jewelry brand online.
**Conversion action:** Complete a checkout / Place an order.
